Know The Mehendi !

Indian Henna

Indian henna uses a finely mixed natural paste applied in detailed, traditional patterns like paisleys and florals. Designs usually cover the full hand and feet, with a lemon-sugar solution applied to enhance the stain and ensure a deep, rich color.

Arabian Henna

Arabic henna features bold floral and leafy designs with more spacing. It's applied using a smooth paste and allowed to dry naturally, resulting in an elegant and striking look that's quicker to apply and perfect for festive occasions.

Western Henna

Western henna is minimal and modern, often using natural or white henna for clean, geometric, or boho-style designs. It's quick to apply, ideal for casual wear or events, and dries fast for temporary yet stylish body art.
