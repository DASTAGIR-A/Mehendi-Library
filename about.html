About Us!

Mehendi Library began with a simple passion for henna art and a deep love for cultural traditions. Founded by a group of friends and artists in Ambur, we started as a small local studio offering mehendi designs for weddings and festivals. Over time, our commitment to quality, creativity, and personalized service helped us grow into a trusted name for mehendi lovers. Today, we proudly offer a range of Indian, Arabic, and Western designs crafted by skilled hands and inspired hearts. Whether it's a grand wedding or a joyful celebration, we bring elegance, tradition, and artistry to every occasion.

What We Offer

At Mehendi Library, we go beyond just beautiful designs. We offer professional mehendi services for weddings, engagements, festivals, baby showers, and casual events. Each design is crafted with precision and a personal touch.

We also sell premium, homemade Henna Cones made from natural ingredients to ensure safe and rich color development. Whether you're a professional artist or a henna enthusiast, our cones are perfect for every use.

Looking for custom designs or planning a special occasion? You can now place orders for our cones or book a mehendi session directly with us!
